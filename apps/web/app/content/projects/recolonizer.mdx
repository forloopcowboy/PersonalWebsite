## Summary

A tower defense built solo in Unity. Pathing, waves, a small economy loop, and the usual late-night arguments with a navmesh.

Side projects are mostly an excuse to keep a beginner's mind near the tools I use professionally — Recolonizer is the C# one. The fundamentals of the genre are well understood; the interesting part is everything around them: pacing, feedback, how a wave feels when it's almost too much.
