## Summary

A platformer built during a game jam. Made in a weekend, walks well enough to keep walking.

The interesting constraint of a jam is that you finish — and finish we did. Together with our rag-tag crew of
creative people, we put together a whole bunch of features, stripped most of them down, narrowed down a fun and
pretty rewarding grappling-hook-platformer experience, and got it out the door just in time.

I am particularly proud – even though I did not work on it – of the hilarious intro cutscene made by Jassi Buum,
our lead artist. Fills me with joy every time I see it.

Give it a try, and see if you can get to the end of the game without dying.
